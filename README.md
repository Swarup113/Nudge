Nudge - Desktop Health Companion

Features
1. Daily Dashboard
Progress Tracking: Visual ring showing your daily health goal completion (Water + Focus sessions).
Quick Actions: Instantly log water or start a focus session without navigating through menus.
Persistent State: Your data is saved locally and persists even after closing the browser.

3. Pomodoro Timer
Focus Sessions: Standard 25-minute focus timer to boost productivity.
Background Running: The timer runs in the background using the Chrome Alarms API. You can close the extension popup, and it will still notify you when time is up.
Session Tracking: Counts your completed focus sessions for the day.

4. Hydration Tracker
Water Logging: Simple +/- buttons to track your 8 glasses a day.
Interval Reminders: Set custom intervals (30m, 1h, 1.5h, 2h) to get nudges to drink water.
Daily History: Logs the time of each glass consumed.

5. Medicine Reminder
Recurring Schedules: Set reminders for daily, twice daily, or weekly medication.
Time-Based Notifications: Get reliable notifications at the exact time you set.

6. Eye Care (Strain Prevention)
20-20-20 Rule: Reminds you every 20 minutes to look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to relax eye muscles.
Blink Reminder: Notifies you every 10 minutes to blink consciously, countering the 66% blink reduction caused by screen use.

7. Posture & Movement
Posture Check: Customizable reminders (default 15m) to straighten your back and fix your sitting position.
Stand Up Alert: Reminds you (default 30m) to stand up and move around to improve blood circulation.
Desk Stretches: Periodic suggestions for simple stretches you can do at your desk.

8. Quick Alarm
One-off Timers: Set a one-time reminder for anything (e.g., "Check oven in 10 mins").
Custom Labels: Add a note to your alarm so you know exactly what to do.
9. Box Breathing
Interactive Guide: A visual guide for the 4-4-4-4 breathing technique used to reduce stress and anxiety.
Visual Feedback: Animated circle expands and contracts to guide your inhale, hold, and exhale phases.
